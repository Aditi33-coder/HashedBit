Flamigo

Flamingos are tall, graceful birds known for their striking pink feathers, long legs, and curved beaks. They get their vibrant color from carotenoid pigments found in their diet of algae and crustaceans. Found in both tropical and subtropical regions, flamingos often live in large colonies near lakes, lagoons, or wetlands.
